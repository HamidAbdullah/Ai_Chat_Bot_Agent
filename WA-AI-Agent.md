## 2. What this system does

The Service:

- Receives **WhatsApp messages** and related events from **Meta** via **webhooks** (HTTPS).
- May send **replies** through **Meta’s WhatsApp Cloud API** using your configured **Phone Number ID** and access token.
- Sends **message content** to **AI providers** (e.g. OpenAI, Google Gemini, Groq, or xAI, depending on your configuration) to generate **automated replies**.

The Service is operated by you or your organization; end users are typically **people who message your WhatsApp business number**.

## 3. Information we may process

Depending on configuration and what Meta sends, the Service may process:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Identifiers** | WhatsApp user IDs / phone numbers (`from`), message IDs (`wamid`), business phone number metadata Meta includes in webhooks. |
| **Message content** | Text of inbound messages; text of outbound replies generated by the Service. |
| **Technical data** | Server logs (timestamps, IP addresses hitting your public URL if you log them), errors, API response metadata. |
| **AI interaction data** | Prompts and model outputs sent to or returned from third-party AI APIs. |

The Service **does not** implement long-term **conversation memory** in the default codebase described in this repository; each reply is based on the **current inbound message** plus the configured **system prompt**, unless you add storage later.
